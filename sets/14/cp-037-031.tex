$I_1= $ Left mesh, clockwise.\\
$I_2= $ Right mesh, clockwise.\\
$I_s= $ Current entering the "black box". \\
A 1-\si{\V} test source exists across the terminals, + reference on top.\\

\begin{align*}
\centering
I_s &= -I_2 \\
V_x &= 2I_2 \\
I_1 &= 2V_x \\
&= 4I_2
\end{align*}

\begin{align*}
\centering
I_2-I_1+2I_2+1 &=0 \\
I_2-4I_2+2I_2+1&=0 \\
I_2 &= 1\qw{\A} \\[2mm]
R_{TH} &= \frac{1\qw{\V}}{I_s} \\
&= -1\qw{\ohm}
\end{align*}
\\[5mm]
